About STEM

STEM is the abbreviation for Science,Technology, Engineering and Mathematics.Since STEM was introduced, it has had a great impact on education, health, industries and the world as a whole.STEM inspires people to explore, learn, and solve complex problems. STEM encourages team work, innovation and critical thinking. STEM makes life easier, it improves innovation and aids in the solving of complex problems.

STEM helps industries or sectors in research, evaluation and innovation. STEM plays a key role in Astronomy, Engineering, Agriculture, Scientific Researches and many more. It has improved the level of productivity in these sectors. Under STEM, we have Robotics, Programming, Web development and others. STEM has played a major role in the development of many counteries and it's sub sectors.
